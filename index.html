About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Southern California (USC) and INRIA in the framework of the Inria@SiliconValley project. I am a member of the DASH group led by Fei Sha and the INRIA Magnet team.

Prior joining INRIA and USC, I was a teaching and research assistant in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at Paris Descartes University. I was a member of the LIPADE laboratory, within the Machine Learning for Data science (MLDS) group led by Professor Mohamed Nadif.

My CV can be found here.
